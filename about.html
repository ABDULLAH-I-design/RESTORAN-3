Preparing a hamburger begins with selecting fresh and high-quality ingredients. The beef is seasoned with salt and spices to enhance its flavor. The meat is shaped into a patty and grilled until it is perfectly cooked. Fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and onions are washed and sliced carefully. Cheese is added on top of the hot patty so it melts nicely.
