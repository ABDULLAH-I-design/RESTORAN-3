Preparing a hamburger begins with selecting fresh and high-quality ingredients. The beef is seasoned with salt and spices to enhance its flavor. The meat is shaped into a patty and grilled until it is perfectly cooked. Fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and onions are washed and sliced carefully. Cheese is added on top of the hot patty so it melts nicely. Soft burger buns are lightly toasted for extra taste. Sauces like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise are spread on the bread. All the ingredients are layered together neatly. The hamburger is served hot and ready to enjoy.
